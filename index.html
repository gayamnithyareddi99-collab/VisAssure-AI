FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the Compliance Trust Gap?

+
It is the gap between the volume of global student applications and the actual operational credibility of those applicants. Admissions teams face high visa refusal rates and severe regulatory risk because manual, subjective mock interviews fail to detect synthetic fraud, rehearsed AI teleprompter coaching, and unverified immigration histories leaving universities exposed to sponsor licence suspension and BCA compliance penalties.

Do I need to replace our existing CRM or admissions software?

+
No. VisAssure AI is platform-agnostic, integrating above your existing stack SITS, Tribal, Salesforce, or any CSV-based workflow so your admissions team keeps familiar tools while gaining enterprise-grade AI compliance intelligence.

How is this different from generic video interviewing tools like Kira Talent?

+
Generic video tools produce a passive recording. VisAssure AI actively evaluates refusal summary intent and clarification where the AI dynamically challenges the student on past visa rejections, analysing cognitive consistency and clarification of intent in real time. It unifies NLP telemetry, biometric deepfake forensics, and post-CAS outcome tracking into a single closed-loop compliance pipeline that no video tool currently offers.

What results have pilot institutions seen?

+
An 8-week pilot across 5 UK professional admissions and pathway provider locations showed a 28% increase in application screening speed, an 18% reduction in post-CAS visa refusal rates across 1,200 tested interactions, and new staff achieving credibility grading proficiency in 14 days versus a historical 45-day average.

Is the platform compliant with UK data and immigration regulations?

+
Yes. The platform is built around Home Office BCA thresholds, UKVI Student Route sponsorship requirements, and ICO guidance on UK GDPR with secure cloud-native architecture, end-to-end encryption, UK-hosted servers, and Cyber Essentials certification. All student data is handled in accordance with strict data protection standards.
